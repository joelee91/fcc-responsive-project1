Timeline of freeCodeCamp

  • Founded and launched in October of 2014 by Quincy Larson
  • Originally featured MEAN (MongoDB, Express.js, AngularJS, Node.js) stack as part of curriculum
  • January 2016, curriculum moves towards providing sources and information from within rather than outsourcing
  • 2016, MEAN stack is then switched out for the MERN (React replacing Angular) stack
  • 2016, freeCodeCamp introduces opportunities to work exclusively with nonprofit organizations
